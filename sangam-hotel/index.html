OUR JOURNEY

More Than Just Coffee

The Daily Grind Cafe started in 2018 with a simple dream: to create a welcoming space where people could connect over exceptional coffee. Our founder, Rohan Sharma, poured his passion into crafting a menu that celebrates local flavors and global coffee traditions, right here in Bengaluru.

We believe in sourcing the finest beans, baking with love daily, and fostering a genuine sense of community in every interaction.
