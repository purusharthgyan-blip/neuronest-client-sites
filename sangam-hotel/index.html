Our Story

A Legacy of Warmth and Hospitality

Established in the heart of Chembur, Sangam Hotel has been a cherished landmark for over two decades. We pride ourselves on creating an inviting atmosphere where every guest feels at home, whether they are here for a quick coffee, a lavish meal, or a comfortable stay.

Our commitment to excellence shines through in our meticulously crafted dishes, serene accommodations, and a team dedicated to making your experience unforgettable. We believe in serving not just food and lodging, but moments of joy and relaxation.
