About Our Cafe

Our Story: Where Passion Meets Flavor

Sarthak Cafe, nestled in the heart of Vashinaka, Chembur, is more than just a coffee shop – it's a community hub where passion for quality meets a love for people. We meticulously source the finest beans and ingredients to craft beverages and dishes that delight your senses.

Come, relax, and savor moments of joy with us. Whether you're catching up with friends, working remotely, or simply enjoying a quiet moment, Sarthak Cafe is your perfect escape.
