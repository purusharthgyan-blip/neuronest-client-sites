Our Story

More Than Just Coffee, It's an Experience

Nestled in the heart of Vashinaka, Chembur, Sarthak Cafe was born from a passion for exceptional coffee and a desire to create a welcoming community space. We believe every cup tells a story, and ours is one of dedication to quality, comfort, and connection.

From our carefully selected beans, roasted to perfection, to our homemade pastries and delightful snacks, we pour our heart into every detail. Come, relax, and savor the moment with us – whether it's for a quick morning pick-me-up or a leisurely afternoon chat.
