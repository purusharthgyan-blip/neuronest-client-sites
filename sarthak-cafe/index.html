Our Story

Sarthak Cafe began with a simple dream: to create a warm, inviting space where the vibrant community of Vashinaka, Chembur could gather over exceptional coffee. We believe in the power of a perfectly brewed cup to brighten days and foster connections.

From carefully selected beans to handcrafted pastries, every detail at Sarthak Cafe is thoughtfully curated to offer you a moment of pure delight. Come, unwind, and become a part of our story.
