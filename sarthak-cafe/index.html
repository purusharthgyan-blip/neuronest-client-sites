Our Story, Your Coffee

Sarthak Cafe was born from a simple passion: to create a warm, inviting space where friends and neighbors in Vashinaka, Chembur can gather, unwind, and savor exceptional coffee. We believe every cup tells a story, and ours is brewed with love, quality ingredients, and a commitment to making your day a little brighter.

From our perfectly pulled espresso shots to our delightful pastries, we strive to offer a moment of joy in your busy schedule. Come in, relax, and let us serve you.
