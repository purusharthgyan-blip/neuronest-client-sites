Our Story: A Taste of Home

Nestled in the heart of Vashinaka, Chembur, Sarthak Cafe is more than just a coffee shop – it's a cozy escape where every sip and bite tells a story. We've been serving delightful brews and fresh treats since 2020, becoming a cherished spot for locals seeking quality and comfort.

Our passion for good food and great coffee drives us to craft experiences that warm your heart and energize your day. Come in, relax, and make Sarthak Cafe your second home.
