OUR JOURNEY

More Than Just A Cafe

Chai & Chat Cafe began with a simple dream: to create a cozy corner where the rich aroma of Indian chai meets the comforting embrace of community. Founded by two friends, Priya and Rohan, our cafe was born from a shared passion for authentic flavors and meaningful connections. We envisioned a space where every cup tells a story and every visit feels like coming home.

Our philosophy is rooted in warmth, quality, and genuine hospitality, ensuring every guest feels cherished from the moment they step through our doors.
